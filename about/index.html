About

An embedded team that took security seriously.

AmbiSecure is the security business unit of Ambimat Electronics — established 1981. We have spent four decades shipping hardware that survives the field. Security is what we add to that DNA, not a marketing layer over someone else’s reference design.

Mission

Safeguard a connected world.

To safeguard our highly connected world against cybercrime — creating safer work and personal environments by pushing trust into hardware, where it cannot be phished, leaked, or quietly replaced.

Vision

Solve the IoT security problem.

The IoT security problem is not one problem — it is identity, attestation, provisioning, key rotation, and secure update, all at the scale of millions of devices. We build the silicon-rooted primitives that make each of those tractable.

Our story

From PCB design to hardware-rooted identity.

Ambimat Electronics started in Ahmedabad in 1981 as an engineering services firm doing PCB design and firmware for industrial customers. Over the decades the team has shipped Bluetooth-based hardware (the Hedron project for Ciright), wearable platforms for Actofit, biometric + GSM security devices for CEEL, prosthetic-control electronics for Robo Bionics, and tens of thousands of smart labels for retail and logistics.

AmbiSecure was carved out as the security-focused practice when our customers began asking the same questions repeatedly: "how do I provision keys at the factory without leaking them?", "how do I make this FIDO-compliant?", "can this card carry a JavaCard applet?" Those are not questions a general embedded team answers casually — they require dedicated practice with Secure Elements, GlobalPlatform, FIDO Alliance specs, and personalisation line tooling. So we built that practice.

Today AmbiSecure ships authenticators, designs JavaCard applets, integrates Secure Elements into IoT and payment hardware, and runs the FIDO Validation Server backend that some of our customers use in production. Our sister property, esim.ambimat.com, focuses on eSIM/eUICC and telecom-grade embedded identity.
