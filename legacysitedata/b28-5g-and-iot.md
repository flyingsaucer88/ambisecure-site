## What is 5G Technology?

Fifth-generation cellular networking offering faster speeds and lower latency than 4G:
- Download speeds approximately 20× faster than 4G (20 GB/s vs. 4 GB/s)
- Data transfer acceleration from 100 Mbps to 10 Gbps and beyond

### Benefits
- Shorter delays enabling real-time connectivity
- Increased connectivity supporting more simultaneous communications
- Faster speeds comparable to fiber-optic networks
- Mobility supporting movement from 0 to 310 mph
- Improved connection density up to 1 million connected devices per square kilometer

## 5G and the Internet of Things

5G supports diverse device connectivity needs and "could enable IoT to run virtually instantaneous traffic analyses, improve security and public safety."

## 5G and Business IoT

Projected to support 22 million jobs globally through digitization of transportation, agriculture, manufacturing, and other sectors.
