## AmbiSecure Bio Enrollment App

> "AmbiSecure Biometric Card enrollment application helps users using a Biometric card to enroll their fingerprints on the card with the help of any NFC enabled mobile device."

The application incorporates "Secure Channel Authentication (SCP02) protocol to first authenticate the card" before fingerprint enrollment via NFC-enabled devices.
