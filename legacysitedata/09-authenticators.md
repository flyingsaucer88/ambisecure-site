## Authenticators

AmbiSecure provides dependable, long-lasting, and secure authentication solutions. The company positions its offering as essential for organizational security enhancement, requiring employee verification beyond standard username and password combinations.

> "Usernames and passwords are vulnerable to brute force attacks and can be stolen by third parties."

## Product Cards Listed

- **AmbiSecure USB Key**
- **Ambisecure One pass card**
- **IoT solution**
- **Ambisecure Bio Card**

The platform leverages "free and open standards from the FIDO Alliance," enabling organizations to replace password-only authentication with secure, expedited login experiences across digital properties.
