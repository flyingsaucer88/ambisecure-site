## What is Tracking & Tracing of Cold Chain Logistics

IoT transforms efficiency in food, pharmaceutical, and perishable supply chains. IoT-driven systems ensure cold chain integrity by automatically adjusting network systems to prevent humidity and temperature threshold breaches in warehouses and trailers. Device-generated data provides real-time audit trails for food safety professionals and helps planners identify root causes of supply chain issues.

For pharmaceuticals, "Cold chain logistics along with pharmaceutical serialization has become the backbone of the quickly developing temperature-sensitive drugs." Track and Trace minimize errors and product loss.

## Benefits of Tracking & Tracing IoT in Cold Chain Logistics

- Real-time tracking of goods
- Temperature monitoring
- Instant alerts
- Route optimization for quick, efficient delivery
- Fuel usage monitoring

Logistics managers access this on personal devices while traveling.

## How Tracking & Tracing data helps Regulatory Requirements

### Electronic Vaccine Intelligence Network (eVIN)
Partnership between India's Ministry of Health and Family Welfare and UNDP, supported by GAVI. "eVIN, a unique innovation that brings together technology, people, and processes to strengthen the vaccine supply chain by digitizing information on vaccine stocks and storage temperatures."

### Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)
FSSAI's Food Products Standards and Food Additives Eleventh Amendment Regulations, 2017 transform the nation's food safety system, requiring sanitary practices for food transport.

### FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)
"The FDA Food Safety Modernization Act is the most significant update of US food safety that directly impacts transportation and the retail and foodservice industries, including registration and record-keeping requirements."

## How Tracking & Tracing systems work

Three main components of cold chain:
1. **Temperature-controlled storage** — refrigerated facilities for cold chain cargo.
2. **Temperature-controlled transport** — insulated cold containers via airways, waterways, roadways, or railways.
3. **Efficient operational and management procedures** — minimizing risk during day-to-day operations.

## Different Types of Systems / Equipment

- **Itemized level tracking** — sensors on trucks/rail/air cargo monitor food temperature.
- **Refrigerated van tracking solution** — businesses monitor shipments 24/7.
- **Inventory Management Tags** — tags, readers, and software to track products entering and leaving cold storage.
- **Pilferage detection Tag** — ensures no pilferage during transportation.

## Use case — F&B / Food Processing Logistics

Manual temperature logging is error-prone — drivers and food service workers may produce inaccurate or missed records ("pencil whipping"). Temperature readings at product arrival don't guarantee maintenance throughout journeys.

> "The U.S. FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA) and Sanitary Transportation of Human and Animal Food (STF), have established minimum standards for packing, transporting, and storing produce."

IoT sensors and automation revolutionize cold chains by capturing real-time data, enabling predictive maintenance and alarms when temperature/humidity deviate.
