## AmbiSecure Tappable

The product enables secure authentication through a single tap, combining door access and payment capabilities in one wearable device for employees.

## Why Choose AmbiSecure Tappable™?

- Improves employee convenience by merging access and payments into one wearable
- Boosts organizational security with enterprise-grade cryptography and centralized IT control
- Supports sustainability by reducing plastic card issuance and enabling reusability across staff cycles
- Enhances brand image with modern, stylish technology for staff and visitors
- Minimizes operational downtime by providing quick re-assignment in case of employee turnover
- Consolidates access cards + payment tokens into one wearable
- Reduces IT management overhead with easy re-issuance
- Enhances employee experience with frictionless authentication
- Stylish, discreet design that suits corporate branding

## Key Features

- **Universal Door Access Compatibility** — Works with HID MultiClass SE, iCLASS SE, and all NXP DESFire-based systems
- **Closed-Loop Payments Ready** — Perfect for campus cafeterias, vending, and transport systems
- **Personalized & Reusable** — Each Tappable is securely provisioned to the user and can be re-assigned by IT when needed
- **Enterprise-Grade Durability** — Built with wash-proof materials to withstand daily use
- **Future-Proof Security** — Backed by AmbiSecure's strong cryptographic frameworks and designed for interoperability with global standards

## Use Cases

- **Universities** — Student ID, dorm access, and cashless dining
