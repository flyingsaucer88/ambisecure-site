## How is AmbiSecure different?

### Strong Authentication
"The Ambisecure Card offers superior security by combining hardware-based authentication and public key cryptography to defend against phishing attacks."

### Passwordless Support
"The Ambisecure Card helps organizations accelerate to a passwordless future with resident credentials by supporting the FIDO2 protocol."

### User Experience
"The Ambisecure Card Series provides a simple and intuitive authentication experience that users find easy to use."

### Battery-free
The card requires no battery, ensuring continuous protection.
