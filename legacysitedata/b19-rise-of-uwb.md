## Introduction

The post introduces Ultra-wideband (UWB) technology as an emerging solution for real-time location tracking across industries. "The increasing need to know the location of resources, such as people, equipment, and materials, has been rapidly expanding."

UWB operates "in a large bandwidth spectrum that is generally greater than 500 MHz" and enables wireless connections at speeds exceeding 110 Mbit/s.

## Ultra-wideband Market and Top Applications / End-user Industries

### Consumer Electronics
Enhances home entertainment connectivity for televisions, laptops, and audio equipment while meeting low-power requirements.

### Healthcare
Tracks hospital assets, patients, and staff. "Hospitals across North America and Europe spend a considerable amount toward replacing lost equipment."

### Retail
Provides real-time location data for products and employees, replacing traditional paper-based tracking systems.

### Manufacturing
Supports asset, tool, and employee tracking; monitors telemetry conditions like humidity, pressure, and temperature.

### Automotive & Transportation
Enables keyless entry, tire pressure monitoring, and navigation systems with wireless data transmission.
