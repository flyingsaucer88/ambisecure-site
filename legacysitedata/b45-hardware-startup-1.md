The post discusses India's emergence as a hardware development hub, supported by the government's #MakeInIndia initiative. The author expresses satisfaction with supporting startups during this nascent phase of the industry.

## Reasons for startup failures

1. Insufficient capital for bootstrapping
2. Inexperience in decision-making regarding product development direction
3. Lack of knowledge about what practices to avoid
4. Inaccurate project timeline and resource estimations
5. Inability to complete prototypes within projected timeframes

The author notes that investor hesitation toward hardware ventures has created a challenging environment for entrepreneurs. Consequently, Ambimat announced plans to launch an article series providing guidance for entrepreneurs on hardware development and commercialization.
