## AmbiSecure Card

> "The Ambisecure Card is a hardware authentication solution that is FIDO2 certificated."

> "This card used to provide a simple and highly secure sign-in feature to any online server supporting the FIDO authentication algorithm and enables compliance requirements for strong authentication."

> "Today passwords are fast becoming obsolete and difficult to remember. Due to the ongoing pandemic, more and more employers prefer to have their employees work from home. Many times they work from places that don't have the necessary security levels and the security of your digital profile, is very often compromised. Hence it is necessary to up the anté with hardware security using technology that is backed by technology leaders like Google and Facebook."

### Strong Authentication
"The Ambisecure Card offers superior security by combining hardware-based authentication and public key cryptography to effectively defend against phishing attacks and eliminate account takeovers. It offers support for FIDO U2F and FIDO2."

### Passwordless Enabled
"The Ambisecure Card helps organizations accelerate to a passwordless future with resident credentials by providing support for the FIDO2 protocol. FIDO2 supports not only today's two-factor authentication but also paves the way for eliminating weak password authentication, with strong single factor hardware-based authentication."

### Easy, Fast, and Reliable
"The Ambisecure Card Series provides a simple and intuitive authentication experience that users find easy to use, ensuring rapid adoption and organizational security with the click of a button. Ambisecure card does not require a battery making sure you never run out of juice to protect your IT infrastructure."
