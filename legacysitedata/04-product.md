## Why AmbiSecure

### Strong Authentication
"The AmbiSecure Card offers superior security by combining hardware-based authentication and public key cryptography to effectively defend against phishing attacks and eliminate account takeovers. It offers support for FIDO U2F and FIDO2."

### Passwordless Enabled
"The AmbiSecure Card helps organizations accelerate to a passwordless future with resident credentials by providing support for the FIDO2 protocol. FIDO2 supports not only today's two-factor authentication but also paves the way for eliminating weak password authentication, with strong single or multi factor hardware-based authentication."

### Easy, Fast, and Reliable
"The AmbiSecure Card provides a simple and intuitive authentication experience that users find easy to use, ensuring rapid adoption and organizational security with the click of a button. AmbiSecure card does not require a battery making sure you never run out of juice to protect your IT infrastructure."
