## Ambisecure OpenID connect

> "The AmbiSecure eSIM solution consists of an OpenID connect Applet that is able to allow clients to verify the identity of the End-User based on the authentication performed by an Authorization Server, as well as to obtain basic profile information about the End-User in an interoperable and REST-like manner."
