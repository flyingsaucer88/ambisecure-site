## Introduction

> "Cold chains are essential to sustain the quality and extend the shelf-life of perishable products, such as seafood, chemicals, frozen foods, and medicinal drugs."

The global cold chain logistics market valuation was USD 159 billion in 2018, with projections to reach USD 585 billion by 2026.

## Three Main Components of Cold Chain

1. Temperature-controlled storage — refrigerated facilities for storing cargo
2. Temperature-controlled transport — insulated containers for various transportation modes
3. Efficient operational and management procedures — to minimize and contain risks

## IoT Solutions

- Smart Labels for temperature monitoring
- Truck tracking solutions for 24/7 shipment monitoring
- Inventory Management Tags (UHF-Tags) for tracking products
- Pilferage detection Tags for supply chain security

## Conclusion

The company highlights "end-to-end visibility of perishable products" addressing temperature monitoring, shipment tracking, and spoilage reduction.
