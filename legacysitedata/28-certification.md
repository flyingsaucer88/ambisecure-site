## AmbiSecure Products are FIDO Certified

AmbiSecure has developed FIDO solutions based on U2F and FIDO 2 protocols through adoption of the international FIDO Alliance standard.

### Three Core Principles of FIDO

- **Stronger** — "goes beyond password and One-Time-Password (OTP) solutions, it provides security by combining hardware-based authentication"
- **Interoperable** — "provides a simple and intuitive authentication experience that users can ensure rapid adoption"
- **Simpler for consumers to use** — "more straightforward for consumers to use the system to perform the tasks safely"

## FIDO2 Overview

FIDO2 represents the newest FIDO Alliance specifications enabling authentication across desktop and mobile devices. It addresses the global password challenge and traditional authentication concerns.

## Organization Solutions

AmbiSecure addresses passwordless login and hybrid environment access through two FIDO2 devices:
- AmbiSecure FIDO Card (Smart Card)
- AmbiSecure FIDO Security Key (USB token)

## FIDO Advantages

- Authentication via cryptographic proof instead of shared secrets
- Strong protection against phishing and MITM attacks
- Easy to use with no client software installation
- Cross-platform support maximization
- Varied product line with multiple form factors
- Interoperability backed by major internet and financial services firms
