## Introduction

The blog introduces IoT solutions applied to cold chain logistics, emphasizing how this technology enables cost optimization and real-time monitoring throughout the supply chain process. The cold chain — a temperature-controlled supply chain — is essential for safely delivering perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals while maintaining strict temperature ranges.

IoT integration allows companies to "consistently connect with every step in the logistical process, allowing for cost-optimizing benefits," enabling analysis of temperature settings and response to climate changes, packaging damage, delays, and human errors.
