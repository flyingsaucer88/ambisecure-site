## Cyber Security Threats in India

> "This new digital workforce has pushed most employees to go online, including video conferencing, which has led to privacy issues and phishing attempts, including ransomware attacks."

Remote work adoption enabled cybercriminals to target employees for confidential data acquisition. Companies struggle providing security to remote workforces.

## Types of Cyber Threats

- Large scale anti-fraud bypass
- ATM malware in financial institutions
- Account-centric frauds (stealing credentials, OTP)
- Phishing (malicious links, malware installation)
- Identity theft from data breaches
- Threats from disgruntled employees
- Ransomware targeting under-resourced banks
- Attacks on unsecured networks and Man-In-The-Middle attacks

## Documented Cyber-Attacks in India

1. **UIDAI Aadhaar** — Billion details leaked in 2018; 210 government websites hacked.
2. **Domino's India** — 180 million pizza orders exposed; 1 million credit card records compromised.
3. **Union Bank of India (2017)** — $170 million stolen via SWIFT access codes.
4. **SBI Data Breach** — Three million SMS messages exposed customer account details.
5. **Police Exam Database** — 500,000 candidates' PII sold; included names, contacts, criminal history.
6. **COVID-19 Test Results** — Thousands of patient reports publicly accessible via Google indexing.
