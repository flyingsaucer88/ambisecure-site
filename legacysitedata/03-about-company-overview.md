## About AmbiSecure

### Solving your security problems.

The digital workforce shift has increased reliance on online services with weak cybersecurity, leading to privacy concerns, phishing, and ransomware. AmbiSecure addresses these threats.

## What AmbiSecure Offers

Two components:

### IoT Solution
Hardware-level security foundation for end-to-end communication in large-scale infrastructure.

### Enterprise Solution
Hardware-based authentication combining intuitive experience with rapid adoption and scalability.

## Why AmbiSecure

- Committed to "security without compromise, securing your enterprise against tomorrow's threat, today"
- FIDO certified products adopting international standards
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) requiring at least two evidence factors: knowledge, possession, or inherence

## Mission

> "To safeguard our highly connected world against cybercrime to create a safer and more secure work and personal environment."

## Vision

> "To solve the IoT security problem."
