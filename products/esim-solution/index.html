eSIM & Identity

AmbiSecure eSIM Solution

Ambimat’s eSIM platform — consumer, M2M, and automotive eUICC under SGP.22 and SGP.32 — with an OpenID Connect applet that lets clients verify the end-user identity through the Authorisation Server. Detailed RSP and profile work lives at esim.ambimat.com.

Why eSIM with identity

When the SIM is also the identity bearer.

OpenID Connect applet

An OpenID Connect applet allows clients to verify the identity of the end-user based on the authentication performed by an Authorisation Server.

Profile retrieval

Obtain basic profile information about the end-user in an interoperable, REST-like manner.

Consumer SGP.22

Standard consumer eSIM RSP. Activation by QR or activation code. Multiple operator profiles.

M2M SGP.32

Machine-to-machine eSIM. Operator-driven profile management. Designed for fleets and IoT.

Automotive-ready

Vehicle eUICC integration via Ambimat’s automotive electronics line.

Same Ambimat group

Shared Ambimat secure-element heritage, shared personalisation lines, shared FIDO MFA for operator portals.
